Frequently Asked Questions

How does the 2-minute website builder work?

Our AI-powered platform analyzes your requirements and automatically generates a custom website based on your preferences. Simply answer a few questions about your business, choose a template, and our system handles the rest.

What's included in the free plan?

The free plan includes hosting, SSL certificate, basic SEO tools, and access to core templates. Premium features like custom domains, e-commerce, and advanced analytics are available in paid plans starting at $9.99/month.

Can I use my own domain name?

Yes! You can easily connect your existing domain or purchase a new one through our platform. We provide free SSL certificates and DNS management tools for all custom domains.

We provide 24/7 live chat support, extensive documentation, video tutorials, and a dedicated customer success team. Premium plans include priority support and one-on-one consultations.

Is my website mobile-responsive?

Absolutely! All websites created with UWB are fully responsive and optimized for all devices - desktop, tablet, and mobile. Our AI ensures perfect display across all screen sizes.
